Tone Analysis Formula Logic

Primary Tone Coherence Score (TCS)
TCS = (∑(tone_strength_i × weight_i) / total_sentences) × coherence_multiplier

Where tone_strength_i represents the strength of tone i across all sentences

Tone Transition Score (TTS)
TTS = 1 - (∑|tone_jump_i| / (total_sentences - 1))

Measures smoothness of tone transitions between sentences

Tone Jump Score (TJS)
TJS = exp(-α × max_tone_jump) × consistency_factor

Where α = 0.5 (exponential decay factor) and max_tone_jump is the largest tone change

Final Coherence Score
Final_Score = (TCS × 0.5) + (TTS × 0.3) + (TJS × 0.2)

Weighted combination of all tone metrics
